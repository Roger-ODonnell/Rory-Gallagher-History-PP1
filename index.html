Rory Gallagher

William Rory Gallagher was an Irish musician and songwriter. He is known for his virtuosic style of guitar playing, and is often referred to as "the greatest guitarist you've never heard of"

Band Members

Gerry McAvoy

John Gerard McAvoy is a Northern Irish blues rock bass guitarist. He played with blues rock musician Rory Gallagher between 1970 and 1991.

Rod De'Ath

Roderick Morris Buckenham de'Ath was a Welsh musician, best known for his role as drummer with Irish guitarist Rory Gallagher in the 1970s.

Ted Mckenna

Edward McKenna was a Scottish drummer who played with The Sensational Alex Harvey Band, Rory Gallagher.

Mark Feltham

Mark Feltham is an English musician, best known for playing harmonica with Rory Gallagher.
